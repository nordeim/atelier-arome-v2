Our Alchemical Process

Where Botany Becomes Poetry

1

Nigredo

Preparation of botanical materials through gentle maceration, allowing the essence to emerge from darkness.

2

Purification through slow distillation, separating the pure essence from earthly impurities.

3

Transformation through careful aging and blending, developing depth and complexity.
