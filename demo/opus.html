Chapter III

The Philosophy of
Intentional Scent

Éloise Durand

Founder & Master Perfumer

In an age of synthetic abundance, we chose the path of botanical truth. Atelier Arôme was born from a singular conviction: that the plants which have accompanied humanity for millennia still hold wisdom we've barely begun to understand.

Our practice draws from the illuminated manuscripts of Renaissance herbalists, the precision of modern analytical chemistry, and the intuitive artistry that transforms ingredients into experience. Every formulation begins with silence—a meditation on intention before the first drop is measured.

We source directly from small-scale distillers across the globe: Bulgarian rose farmers whose families have cultivated the same valleys for generations, Japanese hinoki foresters who understand the seven-year cycle of wood maturation, Malagasy vanilla curers who know each bean by touch.

This is not merely commerce—it is preservation. Each purchase sustains traditional cultivation methods that industrial agriculture would otherwise erase. You become part of a chain of stewardship stretching back centuries.
